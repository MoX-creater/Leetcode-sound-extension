# LeetCode Sound Notifications Extension

## Setup Instructions

1. **Add Your Audio Files**:
   - Go to `\leetcode-sound-extension`
   - You will see two empty placeholder files: `success.mp3` and `error.mp3`.
   - Replace these files with your own audio files. **Make sure they are named EXACTLY `success.mp3` and `error.mp3`**!
   
2. **Install to Your Browser**:
   - Open Chrome or Edge.
   - Go to `chrome://extensions/` (or `edge://extensions/`).
   - Enable **Developer Mode** (usually a toggle in the top right corner).
   - Click on **Load unpacked**.
   - Select the folder: `c:\Users\Lenovo\OneDrive\Desktop\dev\ex\leetcode-sound-extension`.

3. **Enjoy!**
   - Head over to LeetCode and solve a problem. 
   - When you click the **Submit** button, the extension waits for the network to finish.
   - Once the result appears ("Accepted" or "Wrong Answer"/etc), the corresponding sound will play exactly once!
